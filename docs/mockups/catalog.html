Minimoog Model D

Moog Music

Synth 47 owners

The Minimoog Model D is a monophonic analog synthesizer, originally manufactured from 1970 to 1981 and reissued in 2016. Known for its warm, fat bass and lead tones, it uses three voltage-controlled oscillators with a 24 dB/oct transistor ladder filter. A cornerstone of electronic music.
