# Vision — GearAddict

## Problem

Home studio owners accumulate a wide range of gear — synthesizers, effects units, keyboards, audio interfaces — but have no dedicated place to catalog what they own, track its condition and configuration, or connect with others who use the same equipment. Generic forums and social platforms don't map well to gear-centric discussions.

## Solution

GearAddict is an interactive platform for home studio musicians to manage their personal gear inventory and connect with a community around the equipment they own and care about.

Each user maintains a gear collection — a structured inventory of their instruments and studio equipment. From there, they can discover other users with the same gear, read and contribute to discussions about specific devices, and share hands-on experience: tips, known issues, workarounds, and opinions.

## Target Users

Home studio owners who are passionate about their equipment — musicians, producers, and sound designers who want more than a spreadsheet to manage their gear and more than a generic forum to talk about it.

## Core Capabilities

- **Gear inventory** — add, organize, and describe owned equipment (synths, effects, keyboards, interfaces, etc.)
- **Equipment pages** — dedicated pages per device aggregating community experience and discussion
- **Community discussions** — threaded conversations scoped to specific gear items, covering pros, cons, issues, and tips
- **Gear connections** — discover other users who own the same equipment

## Success

A user can catalog their entire studio setup in minutes, immediately find others using the same gear, and contribute to a growing body of community knowledge that makes owning and troubleshooting home studio equipment easier.
