# Use Case: Search Equipment Catalog

## Overview

**Use Case ID:** UC-006
**Use Case Name:** Search Equipment Catalog
**Primary Actor:** Visitor, Home Studio Owner
**Goal:** Quickly locate a specific piece of equipment in the catalog by name or manufacturer
**Status:** Approved
**Requirements:** FR-006

## Preconditions

- The actor has access to the search interface (available site-wide and within the "Add Gear Item" flow).

## Main Success Scenario

1. Actor enters a search term into the equipment search field.
2. System returns a list of matching equipment items ordered by relevance, showing device name, manufacturer, and category.
3. Actor selects the desired equipment item from the results.
4. System navigates to the equipment page for the selected item (UC-007), or — when invoked from the Add Gear Item flow — pre-fills the gear item form with the selected equipment.

## Alternative Flows

### A1: No Matches Found

**Trigger:** The search term yields no results (step 2)
**Flow:**

1. System displays a "No results found" message.
2. Actor refines the search term.
3. Use case continues at step 2.

### A2: Search Term Too Short

**Trigger:** Actor submits a search term shorter than the minimum length (step 1)
**Flow:**

1. System does not execute the search and prompts the actor to enter a longer term.
2. Use case continues at step 1.

## Postconditions

### Success Postconditions

- The actor is presented with a relevant list of equipment items matching their query.

### Failure Postconditions

- No navigation or pre-fill occurs; the search interface remains active.

## Business Rules

### BR-001: Minimum Search Length

Search terms must be at least 2 characters long before a query is executed.

### BR-002: Match Scope

Search matches against device name and manufacturer name (case-insensitive).

### BR-003: Infinite Scroll

The search results list uses infinite scroll with lazy loading: an initial batch of matches is returned immediately, and additional batches are fetched as the actor scrolls toward the end of the list. No page-number controls are shown.
