# Use Case: View Equipment Page

## Overview

**Use Case ID:** UC-007
**Use Case Name:** View Equipment Page
**Primary Actor:** Visitor
**Goal:** Read the specifications and aggregated community experience for a specific piece of equipment
**Status:** Approved
**Requirements:** FR-007

## Preconditions

- The equipment item exists in the catalog.

## Main Success Scenario

1. Visitor navigates to an equipment page (via catalog, search, or direct link).
2. System displays the equipment page containing:
   - Device name, manufacturer, and category.
   - Technical specifications.
   - Number of community owners.
   - A summary of community discussion threads.
3. Visitor reads the page content.
4. Visitor optionally follows a link to the full list of discussion threads (UC-008).

## Alternative Flows

### A1: Equipment Page Not Found

**Trigger:** The requested equipment item does not exist (step 1)
**Flow:**

1. System displays a "Page not found" message.
2. Use case ends.

## Postconditions

### Success Postconditions

- The visitor has read the equipment details and community summary.

### Failure Postconditions

- Not applicable; this is a read-only operation.

## Business Rules

### BR-001: Public Access

Equipment pages are publicly accessible without authentication.

### BR-002: Owner Count

The number of owners displayed reflects users who have added this item to their inventory.
